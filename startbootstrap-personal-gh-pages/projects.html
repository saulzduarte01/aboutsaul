Get to Know Me

I like to do awesome things

I enjoy doing a lot of things that I think are pretty awesome. I train hard—especially boxing and weightlifting—because they push me both physically and mentally. I also like studying and learning new things, especially when it comes to technology, drones, or business. In my free time, I have fun playing video games, listening to music, and coming up with creative project ideas. One of the coolest things I’ve done is snowboarding—I love the adrenaline, the focus it requires, and the feeling of freedom it gives. I like staying productive, but I also enjoy simple moments that help me recharge.

I like music

Music is a big part of my life and something I really enjoy. I like exploring different genres, but what I connect with most are songs that have meaning and emotion behind them. Sometimes I just listen, but other times I find myself thinking about lyrics, melodies, and how music can express things that words alone can’t. It helps me focus, relax, and even get inspired for new ideas. Whether I’m studying, training, or just unwinding, music is always there in the background making everything better.
